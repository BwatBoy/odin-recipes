Old Fashioned Pancakes

Description

These pancakes are a family favorite for generations. They take a total of 15 minutes to cook with 5 minutes of prep time.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flower
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt, or more to taste
  • 1 tablesppon white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, sift together flower, baking powder, salt, and sugar. Mix in milk, egg, and melted butter until smooth
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle. Pour the batter onto the griddle using ~1/4 cup per pancake. Brown on both sides and serve hot
