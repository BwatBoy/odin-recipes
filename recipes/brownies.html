Brek's Boxed Brownies

Description

These brownies require 22-52 minutes to cook depending on pan size with around 5 minutes of preparation.

Ingredients

  • 1 box brownie mix
  • 3 tablespoons water
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 Eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 325°F-350°F depending on chart below.
  2. Grease the bottom of pan.
  3. Stir Brownie Mix, water, oil, and eggs in medium bowl until well blended. Spread in pan.
  4. Bake as directed in chart below or until toothpick inserted 2 inches from side comes out clean.

Cooking Time

  • 13" x 9" pan - 350°F for 22-25 minutes
  • 9" x 9" pan - 350°F for 36-38 minutes
  • 8" x 8" pan - 325°F for 50-52 minutes
